About Dexter Morgan

Dexter Morgan is a multi-purpose Discord bot designed for fun, community engagement, and useful utilities. Whether you’re looking for memes, anime, Dokkan Battle rolls, or AI chat — Dexter’s got you covered.

Command List

  • ,ask – Ask Dexter anything; he’ll respond smartly.
  • ,afk – Set your AFK status with an optional reason.
  • ,ai – Chat with Dexter’s built-in AI personality.
  • ,anime – Get random anime facts or images.
  • ,av / ,banner – Fetch a user’s avatar or banner.
  • ,botinfo – See stats and details about Dexter Morgan.
  • ,buy / ,shop – In-bot currency and item shop commands.
  • ,cc / ,ccboard – Create and view custom commands.
  • ,clash – Engage in fun combat mini-games.
  • ,collection – View your collected characters or items.
  • ,daily / ,bonus – Claim daily rewards.
  • ,embed – Create custom embeds easily.
  • ,event / ,milestone – View server or bot events.
  • ,fakemsg – Generate a fake message for fun.
  • ,firstmsg – Show the first message in a channel.
  • ,img – Search and display random images.
  • ,join / ,leave – Join or leave interactive sessions.
  • ,joke / ,meme – Get a random joke or meme.
  • ,lr / ,ur / ,sparking / ,ultra – Show your Dokkan Battle rarity cards.
  • ,msg – Send a direct message (DM) to a user.
  • ,ping – Check bot latency and speed.
  • ,prog – Show your progress or stats.
  • ,random / ,roll / ,roll5 / ,roll10 – Generate random numbers or character rolls.
  • ,rollhelp – Display help for the roll system.
  • ,rps – Play Rock-Paper-Scissors with Dexter.
  • ,save – Save your data or configurations.
  • ,serverinfo / ,serverinf – Show detailed info about the current server.
  • ,stock – Check available stock in the shop system.
  • ,userinfo – Display detailed information about a user.
  • ,help – Show this help menu.
